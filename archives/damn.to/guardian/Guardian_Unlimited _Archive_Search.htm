 Ivanopulo

Guardian Unlimited

Thursday December 28, 2000

In an article about Russia's computer hackers on November 27, we wrongly referred to Ivanopulo, leader of the cracking group Damn, as the "legendary St Petersburg hacker". In fact, he is a computer cracker who lives in Moscow and his website, http://www.damn.to/, contains nothing that can be used for illegally registering software products and does not supply false credit card details.
