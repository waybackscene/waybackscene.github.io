definition : Crackmes are challenges coded by a reverse engineer to test other 'crackers' skills and there are different crackmes. Keygenmes, Crackmes, Keyfileme, Unpackme. Unpackmes are 'Packed' or 'Protected' by either a famous Packer or a Protector and the cracker has to restore the binary to its original format.
