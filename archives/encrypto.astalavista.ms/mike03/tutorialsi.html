What this site is About

I am hoping you know something about Reverse Engineering.

Reverse engineering is the art of finding what secrets a binary might hold.

Reverse engineering can be used for finding and fixing bugs in a binary.

Also RE ( Reverse engineering ) can be used for "illegal" purposes.

What the following tutorials you might see is for the sole reason of education ONLY.

If you want to find out how Reverse engineers go about reversing a binary here you can find some of the basic steps taken.

And this site is used to host some tutorials by me. admittingly not the best or properly explained but it may help others to motivate themselves to learn by themselves.
