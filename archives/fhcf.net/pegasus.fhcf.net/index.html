 Website "done" - 09.11.2005

 Pegasus helps software company - 18.09.2005
Pegasus, or more precisely FHCF, was asked to stop cracking a particular software. We agreed to this, and decided to leave the company alone in the future. The next day we received another email from the company which then had a favor to ask. "Can you help us protect our software better?" Our answer was yes, and the job is now done. Nothing can be made uncrackable, but I hope our tips were of any use. Good luck in the future!
