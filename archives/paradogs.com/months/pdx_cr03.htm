Paradox Trainermenu Sourcecode

Here is the full sourcecode of Icarus' Paradox trainermenu made during his time in the N64 console section of Paradox. Due to the fact that Icarus was a little bit bored about the N64-scene he decided to slow down his N64 activities in his old group "Lightforce" while he is still in full effect in the PSX-section of Paradox.

Icarus is on the keyboard...

So here is the full sourcecode of my Paradox trainermenu. It is not optimized at all and I know it.. So if you want to complain better talk to a wall instead to me.

I thought it's maybe useful for dudes who might want to know how the asm stuff works or look like, and since Titanik released some of his own sources, i decided to release this to the public, too.

Here is a short description of the files included in archive:

back.raw    : Its the background pic (in BGR 16Bit..)
compile.bat : start it.. itg will compile the intro to .v64
copper      : this is the copperbar i made for the scroller
header      : a standard header for N64 roms
ias2.exe    : IAS2 - a cool tool written by Silo/Blackbag!
intro.asm   : guess what.. the main source
pdyfnt.raw  : the font - i ripped it from an amiga-intro
readme.txt  : guess
runcart.exe : For preparing the compiled stuff for .v64
scroll.txt  : the scrolltext

Maybe it will be useful for some people and it will bring some more action and active people into the scene.
