Customer Service or technical helpdesk?

Do you have problems to apply patches, cracks and trainer versions correctly? Do you have hardware problems? Are you looking for patches, trainers, cracks or missing files?

Once for all: we simply can not help you to solve this quest. It's wasted time approaching us with such kind of topics. Some of you might have forgotten that this is a hobby for us and definately not a profession. We can not answer those questions and to be honest, we are not interested in answering them at all. It might sound arrogant but all of us have for sure much better things to do in both real life and during our scene sparetime.

Always remember: a game worth playing is a game worth buying.

Yes, it might be sound a little bit irritating, but if you are facing trouble in the quest of applying patches correctly simply buy the original version and it will work fine - or not.
And in case you have problems you can contact a customer service of the distributor or even the salesmen of your local game store. Those persons will gladly help you since they are getting paid for it. Support the software industry instead of annoying us! Thank you for reading.
